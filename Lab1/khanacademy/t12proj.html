Borscht soup

  • Time: 35 min
  • Serves: medium
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 400-450 Grams
Potatoes 4 pieces
Onions 1 piece
Carrots 1 piece
Beetroot 3 pieces
Cabbage 1/3-1/2 pieces
Garlic 2-3 cloves
Tomato paste 2 Tbsp.
Vinegar 1 Tbsp.
Salt,pepper,vegetable oil 1 spoon
Water 3-4 Liters
Laurel leaf 1 piece

Step 1: Here is a set of ingredients we need to prepare classic borscht on meat broth

Step 2: First of all, you need to boil meat broth. Wash the meat, put it in a pot and pour water. Add a bay leaf. After boiling, turn the heat down to low and cook until the meat is soft (the time depends on what kind of meat you have chosen). Carefully remove it from the broth when it is ready

Step 3: Strain the broth if desired. Salt to taste and place the diced potatoes. Cook for about 10-12 minutes

Step 4: Peel onions and carrots, chop and fry in a frying pan with a little vegetable oil

Step 5: Place the fried vegetables and finely chopped cabbage in a pan with broth

Step 6: In the meantime, pour a little more oil into the frying pan and add the grated beet. Fry for a couple of minutes and add the vinegar. Stew for another 5 minutes and then add the tomato paste. Stew on low heat for another 5-7 minutes

Step 7: Put the meat into the broth, having previously cut it into portioned pieces, and beet

Step 8: Add salt, pepper, sugar and garlic to taste. Cook for another 7-10 minutes until the potatoes are ready. Then remove from the heat, cover with a lid and leave to infuse for half an hour. Before serving, you can add herbs and sour cream. Borscht is ready! Bon appetit!

Source: Самый вкусный борщ

Dumplings

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: medium
Ingredients Quantity
Wheat flour 500 g
Egg 1 pc.
Water 200 ml
Salt 1/2 tsp.

Step 1:Pour the flour into a bowl. If you have a sieve, you can sift it - this will oxygenate the flour and make the dough more airy.

Step 2:Add salt, egg and part of the water. Knead the dough with your hands until homogeneous. If the dough does not turn out to be sufficiently steep, you can add a little more water.

Step 3:Wrap the dough in cling film and let it rest for half an hour.

French fries

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: medium
Ingredients Quantity
potatoes 500 g.
salt ½ tbsp.
sugar 1 tbsp.
water 4 l.
vegetable oil 800 ml

Step 1: Wash and peel the potatoes. If you want the dish to look like a restaurant dish, cut off the rounded sides, giving the tubers a cube shape.

Step 2:Slice the potatoes into bars no more than 1cm wide. This will help them to roast more evenly. The oblong slices will be easier to eat and dip into the sauce. Rinse the sliced potatoes again, you will see the water turn cloudy because of the starch.

Step 3:Boil the water in a saucepan, drop in the potato bars. Once the water has come back to the boil, turn off the heat immediately and drain the potatoes in a colander. Rinse the bars under cold water. The sudden change of temperature will shorten the time of frying the potatoes in oil and will help to achieve the desired result: crispy crust and soft core.

Step 4: Prepare a deep bowl with cold water, dissolve 1 tbsp. sugar and 0.5 tbsp. salt in it. Soak the potatoes in the water for 5-7 minutes. This method is used in many fast-food restaurants when preparing preparations.

Step 5:Spread a kitchen towel on a flat surface, lay the potatoes out on it and dry them thoroughly with paper towels. It's important that the bars are completely dry so they won't stick together when frozen.

Step 6:Arrange the potatoes in portioned cellophane bags and place in the freezer. It will take 5-6 hours to completely freeze. You can make a few bags ahead of time and then take them out of the freezer and make your favorite fries.

Step 7:In a saute pan or thick-walled skillet, heat the vegetable oil. If you dip one potato into the oil, it will bubble immediately, so you're ready to fry.

Step 8:Cook the potatoes in small batches for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally with a metal skewer.

Step 9:Transfer the potatoes to a paper towel to absorb excess oil. Just before serving, sprinkle the fries with salt and serve with your favorite sauces.
